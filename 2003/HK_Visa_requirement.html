Remarks :

  • * All nationals are required to hold a valid visa for the HKSAR for whatever purpose (including those who are in transit and remain on the airside).
  • ^ All nationals (except holders of Diplomatic and Official passports) are required to hold a valid visa for the HKSAR for whatever purpose (including those who are in transit and remain on the airside).
  • @ Holders of Portuguese Aliens passports and who have held Macau identity cards for not less than two years do not require visas or entry permits for a visit not exceeding seven days.

The information in this chapter on visa or entry permit requirements for visitors to the HKSAR is updated in April 2003. There may be subsequent changes. Please refer to the official copy for more up-to-date information.
