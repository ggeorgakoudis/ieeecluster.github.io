Cluster 2003: Poster Information 

The poster session will be held during the reception on Tuesday, 2 December from 5:30 pm to 7:30 pm.

Here are the specific details for the session:

  • We will provide a poster display board that is of size 1m x 2m, and we will supply the necessary stationaries.

  • We have prepared a series of poster templates (in ppt format) to aid the poster design. There is space for you to enter the title of the poster paper and the authors' names. The rest is for placing your presentation materials. Roughly speaking, the space is large enough for six A4-size presentation notes. [Template1]  [Template2] [Template3] [Template4]
  • We could provide the printing service if authors send in their completed design of the poster on or before 15 November, 2003. Please send your completed design by email to cluster2003@csis.hku.hk. If the filesize of the completed poster design is too large to be handled by email, please send us the URL and we can download it from your site.
  • If we do not receive your completed poster design, we will print a blank poster with your paper title and the authors' names on it. Authors could simply prepare their presentation materials on six A4-size notes, and stick their notes on top of the printed poster.
