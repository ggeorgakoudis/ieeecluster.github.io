General Registration Information
To qualify for advance registration discounts, registration form and payment must be received by 15 November 2003. Registrations received after the deadline will be charged at the late/on-site registration rate. Those received after the late deadline may not be acknowledged or accepted and may require processing and payment onsite.

Registration Confirmation
Email confirmation of conference registration will be sent by the Cluster2003 Financial Chair within seven days of receiving your registration. If you do not receive confirmation, please email us at cstschan@comp.polyu.edu.hk. Please reference Cluster 2003 conference.

Member and Student Member Discounts
To qualify for the discounted Member rates, you must be a member of IEEE or the IEEE Computer Society, and include your membership number(s). To qualify for the Full-time Student rate you must be a student who is attending university on a full-time basis. A copy of student ID card or a letter from the student’s professor must be submitted together with the completed registration form. Non-members can save immediately by applying online now for IEEE membership or IEEE Computer Society membership.

Invitation Letters
Our policy is that invitation letters can be issued only to speakers, presenters, committee members, IEEE members or persons known by the organizing committee. Registration fees must be settled before the letter is provided. Letters will only be sent by Postal Mail or fax and only to the individual registered for the conference. To request for the invitation letter, please contact the Program Chair Dr. C.L. Wang at clwang@csis.hku.hk.

Payment
Payment must accompany all registration forms and can be made by credit card (MasterCard or VISA), check, or bank draft. Checks or bank drafts must be in HK dollar, and payable to HK SECT OF IEEE (COMPUTER CHAPTER). No registrations will be accepted over the phone. Forms received without actual payment WILL NOT be processed.

On-Site Registration
On-site registrants will pay late registration fees. Credit cards (MasterCard or VISA) are the preferred form of payment. All checks must be in HK dollar, and payable to HK SECT OF IEEE (COMPUTER CHAPTER).

Cancellations and Substitutions
Cancellations and substitutions are allowed. However, full registration fees will be charged unless a cancellation notice is sent in writing, and received or faxed on or before 15 November to:

Cluster 2003 Conference
c/o Dr. Alvin Chan
Department of Computing
The Hong Kong Polytechnic University
Hung Hom, Hong Kong

Fax: (852) 2170 0103

A HK$780 (~USD 100) handling fee will be applied to all canceled registrations. Fees cannot be refunded for registrations canceled after 15 November 2003. No-shows will be billed. (Note: At least one author of an accepted paper must register at the full rate, any cancellation request that violates this requirement will not be approved.)
For substitution, a letter on your company's letterhead is required from the original registrant stating the conditions of the substitution and the name of the replacement. Contact the Organizer of the Cluster 2003 Conference at the above address for substitutions (allowed at any time).
