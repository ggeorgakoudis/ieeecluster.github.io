Sheraton Hong Kong Hotel & Towers

The Sheraton hotel is located in the heart of Kowloon, Tsim Sha Tsui, the main shopping area in Hong Kong. It is close to the waterfront and is within 5 mins walk to the MTR station and 10 mins walk to the Star ferry. Opposite to Sheraton, you will find the popular landmarks of Tsim Sha Tsui, such as the Space Museum, the Hong Kong Cultural Centre, and the old Clock Tower. The hotel has 780 guest rooms and suites, all are luxurious and comfortable. The rooms offer either splendid views of the harbour or of the city.

Room Facilities:
Air conditioning. Television. Satellite television. In-house movie. Radio. Direct dial phone. Mini bar. Hairdryer. Voltage 220v.

Hotel Facilities:
Large sized lobby. 24-hour room service. 1 (heated) outdoor pool. High speed Internet Access. Gymnasium. Sauna. Shop. Boutique. Travel agency facilities. Car rental facilities. Baby sitting. Laundry facilities. Disabled facilities.
