Invitation

We would like to invite you to participate in Cluster 2003 - a great event that will bring people from all over the world that are interested in Cluster Computing. The 2003 IEEE International Conference on Cluster Computing, from 1 - 4 December 2003, is a flagship event for cluster researchers and system builders, as well as cluster users, to present and discuss new directions and results that could shape the future of cluster computing. In recent years, the conference has attracted increasing interest from academics, scientists and professionals working in the Asia-Pacific, Europe and North America.

Cluster 2003 will be held in the most fascinating city in the Orient - Hong Kong. The city is the businesses and information hub of Asia, as well as is one of the important gateways to Mainland China. Being the first time this great event is taking place in Asia, as a sponsor and/or exhibitor, participating in Cluster 2003 conference gives businesses both visibility and marketing opportunities. In addition, this event will be the major forum of the year at which you can contribute by sharing your research results and announce new developments.
