The Cluster 2003 proceedings will be published by IEEE Computer Society. The camera-ready paper (electronic form by FTP upload) AND copyright form (by fax) should be sent to the IEEE CS before September 21, 2003. Detailed instructions regarding the paper format and submission procedure has been sent to you by IEEE CS on August 30, 2003.

Author Registration for each accepted paper

The author registration will be handled by Cluster2003 conference. For your full paper or poster paper to be included in the proceedings, AT LEAST ONE of the authors needs to send in a completed registration form along with payment of registration fees to our Finance/Registration chair, Dr. Alvin Chan, before September 21, 2003. If the registration is not received by this date, the paper will not be included in the conference proceedings. Authors with multiple papers appearing in the proceedings must pay the full registration fee plus extra page charges (if any) for each accepted paper. Please note that the student discount cannot be applied to meet the author registration requirement.

Each conference registration includes one copy of the proceedings, allows you to admit to the technical sessions, poster sessions, daily refreshment breaks, reception, and all social functions.

Camera-ready manuscript

Manuscripts to be presented at the conference and published in the proceedings of the conference must be submitted in the format prescribed.

  • Authors are allowed up to 8 pages (for a full paper) or 4 pages (for poster paper) in the proceedings for each paper. You may buy up to two additional pages at a cost of HK $780 (~USD 100) per page. Extra Page charge should be SEND along with the Registration Form.

  • Special Note : Please send a doc (MS Word) version of your final manuscript, only. Do not send a pdf version of your paper. If you prepare your paper using Latex, please output your Latex file to postscript, and send the .ps version. You may also output to postscript and send the .ps file if you are using Word, or some other application. The IEEE CS Press no longer accepts PDF files due to font incompatibility issues. If you have any questions, please contact the editor, Mr. Bod Werner, directly.

  • Final manuscript submission: If using a web browser, please submit your paper electronically to IEEE FTP site. The paper should be uploaded at:
    ftp://ieeecs@ftp.computer.org/press/incoming/proceedings/cluster03/

  • File name: Please name your file using your paper code and your name, per this sample:
    <paper_number_last_name_first_initial.ps>
    For information about your paper code number, please take a look at the following lists: regular papers and poster papers.

  • LaTex Macros: LaTex formatting macros are available on IEEE ftp site at:
    ftp://pubftp.computer.org/Press/Outgoing/proceedings/
    Please select the filename: <LaTex macros.zip>, which is for the 8.5 x 11 Proceedings format.
