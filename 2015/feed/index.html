They are drawn from many different domains including universities and other academic organizations, industries, user communities, and government-operated laboratories.

This prestigious event will bring together key researchers, practitioners and decision makers in Cluster Computing from all over the globe to:

  • promote technical interaction
  • identify key open problems and challenges
  • foster better research collaboration

This conference will feature:

  • keynote presentations by experts from North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific
  • peer reviewed selected papers presentation sessions
  • two panels and three keynote talks
  • a set of tutorials on various aspects of the state of the art
  • an industry exhibition opportunity
  • a number of workshops exploring new and emerging themes

All papers are to be published in a proceedings by the internationally renowned Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers — IEEE. It is expected that approximately 200-250 people will be attending the conference.

Sponsorship Options

We are inviting sponsorship from industries, businesses, and government agencies including the leading players in Cluster Computing. Various levels of sponsorship are possible. Based on the sponsorship level, the sponsor will have access to opportunities for advertising and/or displaying their produces, making presentations and a number of free registrations to symposium. We are open to other suggestions from sponsors as well.

We have a number of sponsorship opportunities and I would be happy to work with you on what works best. Here are some examples using dollar figures from existing and previous sponsorships:

  1. Bronze sponsorship.  The sponsor would be recognized as a “bronze sponsor” on the conference website, during organizer presentations, and in printed material
    given to attendees. (Intended level $2000)
  2. Silver sponsorship.  The sponsor would be recognized as a “silver sponsor” on the conference website, during organizer presentations, and in printed material given to attendees.  In addition, silver sponsors also get space to display promotional material and interact with attendees.  (Intended level $3500)
  3. Gold sponsorship.  The sponsor would be recognized as a “gold sponsor” on the conference website, during organizer presentations, and in printed material given to attendees.  In addition, gold sponsors also get space to display promotional material and interact with attendees, and give a presentation on some of the new technologies developed by the company in our industry session.  (Intended level $5000)
  4. Diamond sponsorship.  The sponsor would be recognized as a “diamond sponsor” on the conference website, during organizer presentations, and in printed material given to attendees.  In addition, diamond sponsors also get space to display promotional material and interact with attendees, and give a presentation on some of the new technologies developed by the company in our industry session and a more detailed presentation as a half-day tutorial.  (Intended level $10000+)

For all sponsorship levels, the sponsors’ logos will be prominently displayed at all conference events, on the conference web site, as well as in any conference materials. All sponsorships include one or more complimentary registrations for sponsor representatives, depending on the level of sponsorship.
