  • Registration fee has to be paid before the deadline for early registration; otherwise your registration fee will be updated to the current one.
  • If student registration is selected please provide the proof of this category; otherwise you will be requested to pay the registration difference amount.
  • Full payments are required before the conference start. A confirmation voucher and invoice will be sent right after we received the payment. Please review it carefully and take note of your registration number. This number will be requested for any change.
  • All payments have to be made in Euros. Available payment methods are:
    • Credit Card Online: you will be redirected to a Bank secure page. VISA, Mastercard and American Express are accepted.

  • All pending amount has to be paid at the Technical Secretariat during the documentation delivery. In case of bank transfer payment or another method but not received it, you will be requested for an advice bank slip. Otherwise, you will be requested to pay the pending amount.
  • Hotel reservations will be booked on a first-come first-served basis upon reception of the corresponding payment.
  • Grupo Pacífico has block-booked rooms for the dates of the Congress. Previous dates or late departures are subject to hotel availability.
  • In case that the selected hotel was fully booked, we will assign you an available hotel with similar characteristics.
  • All rates are per room per night, including VAT (subject to change according to official order) and breakfast.
  • We advise all participants to request their hotel reservations as soon as possible to guarantee accommodation, not later than August, 4th, 2014.
