Invided Talk

Computational Astrophysics on Superclusters

Professor Matthew Bails, Swinburne Astrophysics  and  Supercomputing, Australia

The Swinburne Supercluster is a 66 Gflop cluster running Digital Unix (Tru64). Motivation for development of the cluster, the types of astronomical
applications that are well-suited to clusters, and tools that help  astronomers use the facility will be discussed
