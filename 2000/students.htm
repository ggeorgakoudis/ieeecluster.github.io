Students Forum

December 1st 2000

What is the Students Forum?

The forum provides students with the possibility to present their new but still uncompleted ideas and thoughts in the field of Cluster Computing within the scope of an international conference. The Students Forum will run in parallel to the main events of the conference. Consequently, other conference's participants can also attend the lectures and subsequent discussions.

It is the organizers' hope that this will help to develop new insights and contacts.

Who can participate at the Students Form?

Generally, everybody who is an undergraduate and who wants to represent his work and his ideas in the field of Cluster Computing is encouraged to take part at our Students Forum. However, if you're a postgraduate or a Ph. D. student you can also take part at our conference. We will decide this from case to case.

How can I participate at the Students Forum?

Please send an email to ralph.schlosser@informatik.tu-chemnitz.de and give a short description of your current work and what you want to present at our forum. You will be informed about your registration.

Important Note: Please have a look at the section "Key dates" below!

Will there be a scholarship program?

Yes, there will be a scholarship program to support student registration and attendance. We are at this time able to support seven students, but this number could change.

Important Note:There is no legal claim for this offer.

Key dates

  • Registration deadline: 31st of October. Please register as early as possible!

  • The Students Forum will take place on 1st of December, at noon

If you have any questions regarding the Students Forum, feel free to send an email to ralph.schlosser@informatik.tu-chemnitz.de. I will try to answer your questions as soon as possible.

Suggestions are also welcome!
