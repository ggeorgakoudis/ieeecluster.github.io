Last-Minute Unreviewed Posters

The posters listed above have been selected by the program commitee. A two-page paper of each is included in the conference proceedings. Beyond that eight authors will have the possibility to present their work during the poster sessions in the "Last-Minute Unreviewed Posters" section. These posters will not be reviewed by the program commitee, nor will they be included in the proceedings. The places are given away in first-come-first-serve order. If you are interested, please contact Friedrich Seifert.
