Please read the following descriptions for the online registration carefully:

The online registration requires a SSL connection. If you are not able to register online (e.g. because your browser does not support SSL), please send us a mail. Using a secure connection is necessary for a safe transmission of your data.
You will have to provide us with the following information in order to register for the Conference.
All fields marked with a star are required for registration.

I. Personal Information
Name:your last name (required)
First Name: your first name (required)
Company/Organization: your company or organization (required)
Street/City: your adress (required)
State: your state (not required)
Zipcode: (required)
Email: your valid e-mail adress (required), it is required, because we will send you an acknowledgement e-mail or additional information
without a valid e-mail adress, you will not be able to register online
URL: the adress of your homepage (not required)
Phone/Fax: in case of any problems (not required)
Job Title: Your position in your company, office, university, etc. (not required)

II. Membership Information
Are you a: Inform us whether you are an IEEE-Member, a full time student (valid student-certificate will be required - send it to us by fax (# +049 (0) 371 531 1806)) or Non-IEEE-Member. This information will be necessary to calculate your registration fee.
IEEE Membership No.: If you are an IEEE-Member, please tell us your IEEE Membership Number.
Students Forum: If you are a student you might want to participate in the students forum

III. Hotel and Flight Information
Hotel: Tell us if you want our partner "Chemnitz Stadtmarketing" to book a hotel room for you. You only need to tell us the category you want (4, 3, or 2 star hotel). This company will contact you for further information.

The prices for the hotel rooms are as following (including breakfast and tax):
Dorint Parkhotel (****):
Single Room 153.00DM, Double Room 87.50DM per person
Sea Side Hotel (***):
Single Room 109.00DM, Double Room 69.50DM per person
Europa Hotel (**):
Single Room 99.00DM, Double Room 64.50DM per person
Flight: Inform us if you want our partner "First Travel Management" to book a flight from your nearest airport to Chemnitz. You only have to tell us the category you prefer (first, business or economy class) and whether you want round trip or just one way. We also need to know you departure airport. "First Travel Management" will then contact you for further information and make a price quote to you.
Shuttle-Service: If you book your flight on your own and you are arriving in Dresden by airplane, our partner "First Travel Management" can arrange a shuttle transfer for you to take you to Chemnitz. Please inform us if you want to make use of this offer and we will forward this information to First Travel Management. They will contact you for further information.

You may also contact First Travel by your own via email for making arrangements concerning your travel.

Dates of arrival/departure: This information is neccessary for hotel and flight registrations and for us to determine the capacities. If you want to stay longer, please mention that in the comment field.

IV. Cultural Events
Please tell us in which cultural events you would like to participate. All events are included in the regular conference fee. Please tell us anyways if you want to attend, so we can provide sufficient capacities.

V. Tutorials

click here for schedule
We offer a number of tutorials on Tuesday 11/28/2000. There are additional fees. Please check whether you are going to attend a tutorial or not.
You may also only attend the tutorial(s) without attending the entire conference. In that case you will not have to pay the Conference Registration Fee, only the tutorial fee has to be paid.
However, if you have registered for Tutorials only, you won't receive the proceedings of the Cluster 2000 and you cannot attend the social events (except for the welcome snack on tuesday).

VI. Additional Pages in Paper
If your final camera-ready paper which you perhaps submitted for the Cluster2000 Conference exeeds the regular limit of 8 pages, we offer you the opportunity to combine the extra fee (50 US$ per extra page, max. 2 extra pages) with your regular registration. Just select the amount of extra pages of your paper in the online registration form. The extra fee will be added to your registration fee automatically.
Important: Only one author of a paper (preferrably the main author) has to make a selection there!
If your paper has extra pages, but you want to decouple this extra fee from the general registration, just select "No Extra Pages" and inform us via the comment field.
