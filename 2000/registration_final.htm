!!!Online Registration has been closed!!!

Now, since there are only a couple of days until the conference the online registration has been closed.

If you want to attend the CLUSTER2000 conference you must register on-site at the registration desk in the conference building. Nevertheless we would like to ask you to send us a short email to cluster2000@tu-chemnitz.de so that we are prepared a bit for your arrival.

Registration Desk

The registration desk will be open for the following times:
Tuesday 11/28/2000 08:00 - 18:00
Wednesday 11/29/2000 08:00 - 18:00
Thursday 11/30/2000 08:00 - 12:00
You cannot miss the registration desk when you enter the conference building through the main entry.

Please also note that you must count with several restrictions for late on-site registrations. This especially applies to the major social event of the conference, the "API Tour of Erzgebirge", where it may be possible that you cannot join it. Please understand that we have to calculate with limited reserves. These limited reserves will be allocated on a first-come first-serve basis for attendees that register on-site.

The on-site registration fees are the same ones as for late registration:
IEEE-Members: 300 US$ or 690 DM
Please be prepared to confirm your IEEE membership at the registration desk!
Non-IEEE-Members: 390 US$ or 890 DM
Full-Time Students: 150 US$ or 350 DM
Please be prepared to confirm your status as a full-time student at the registration desk!

As payment for on-site registrations only cash in US-Dollars or German Marks or VISA/Master Card will be accepted!
If you want to pay your registration fee in cash, please have the right money available, if possible.
