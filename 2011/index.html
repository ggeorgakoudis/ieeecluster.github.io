Cluster 2011 Welcome

Welcome to IEEE Cluster 2011. The conference will be held at the Hilton Hotel in Austin, TX, and hosted by the Texas Advanced Computing Center.

Scope

Cluster 2011 welcomes original unpublished paper submissions from researchers in academia, industry, and government, describing innovative research in the field of cluster and high-performance computing.

Major topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Systems Design and Configuration
  • Tools, Systems Software, and Middleware
  • Algorithms, Applications and Performance
  • Storage and File Systems

This year's conference will also include Poster, Panel, Workshop, and Tutorial sessions for face-to-face interactions with the technology leaders in cluster computing.
