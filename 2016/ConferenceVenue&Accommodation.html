Palais de Chine Hotel

Conveniently situated at the city’s transportation hub, Palais de Chine is minutes away from the newly appointed Taipei Bus Terminal, Taiwan Railway, Taiwan High Speed Rail (HSR), Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) and Taoyuan International Airport MRT (opening in 2015/2016). With Q Square and Vieshow Cinemas nearby, guests staying in Palais de Chine enjoy a variety of shopping and entertainment extravaganzas. Neighboring attractions are within walking distance: including National ­eater Concert Hall, National Museum of History, Longshan Temple, Snake Alley and Ximending. ­The world-renowned National Palace Museum and Taipei 101 can be reached within 15 minutes by taxi.
