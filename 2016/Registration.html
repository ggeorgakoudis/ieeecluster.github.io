Registration Policy

  • Registration Acknowledgement will be sent to you via email within three days after a completion of your online registration. Participants will receive a confirmation of registration and a final confirmation 1 month before the conference begins.
  • The registration fee shall be paid in New Taiwanese Dollars. All amounts will be converted on a fixed currency exchange rate of USD 1: NTD 32.5.
  • To qualify for the IEEE Member rate, please indicate your IEEE membership number.
  • The student registration rates are applicable only to full-time students. Student must provide a copy of student ID card.
  • Upon completion of payment, participants may request invitation letters from the IEEE Cluster 2016 Registration Office for visa purpose.
  • Should you have any further questions or wish to receive additional explanation regarding specific matters concerning congress registration, please do not hesitate to contact IEEE Cluster 2016 Registration Office.
  • No upload of a proof of student status is required. Student badges will show student status.
  • Online registration will be possible until Aug. 28, 2016. After Aug. 28, 2016: please register onsite during the conference.
Cancellation & Refund All cancellations and refund requests must be made by email to the IEEE Cluster 2016 Registration Office. For cancellations postmarked on or before 23:59 (GMT+8), Aug. 22, 2016, 70% of the registration fee will be refunded. For cancellations on or after 00:01 (GMT+8), Aug. 22, 2016, no fees will be refunded. All refunds will be issued after the conference. You will receive a final confirmation by e-mail with more detailed information 3 weeks prior to the meeting.
