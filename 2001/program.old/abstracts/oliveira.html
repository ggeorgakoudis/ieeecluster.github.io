DECK-SCI: High-Performance Communication and Multithreading for SCI Clusters 

Fábio A. D. de Oliveira
fabreu@inf.ufrgs.br 
Federal University of Rio Grande doSul
César A. F. De Rose
Catholic University of Rio Grande do Sul 
Rafael B. Ávila
Federal University of Rio Grande doSul

This paper presents the design and implementation of DECK-SCI, a
multithreaded communication library that fully exploits the
high-performance capabilities of the SCI technology. We compare
DECK-SCI, in terms of performance, to a commercially distributed MPI
implementaion and to a freely available MPICH distribution, both
specifically designed for SCI clusters.
