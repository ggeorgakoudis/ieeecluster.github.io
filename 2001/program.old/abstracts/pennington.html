Terascale Linux Clusters at the NCSA

Rob Pennington
NCSA

The NCSA is operating two terascale Linux clusters,
a 512 node PIII cluster and a 160 node Itanium cluster,
as computing and research platforms. They are also being
used to prepare for the McKinley-based clusters that will
be part of the NSF-funded Distributed Terascale Facility.
The TeraGrid clusters, located at the NCSA, SDSC, Argonne
National Lab and Caltech, will be linked by a 40 Gb/s
network and have almost 600 TB of online storage 
and over 13 TF of computing power.
