IEEE Cluster 2001: Speaker Guidelines 

Speakers will be allowed 30 minutes for each talk, except keynote speakers who will be allowed 1 hour. We will reserve the final 5 minutes of each talk for questions, so the talks themselves should be 25 minutes (55 minutes for keynotes).

Electronic projectors that can be attached to laptops and overhead projectors will be supplied for each speaker. If anthing else is needed, please contact Nalini Venkatasubramanian.
