An efficiency and scalability models for heterogeneous clusters 

Luis Pastor
lpastor@escet.urjc.es
Jose L. Bosque
Universidad Rey Juan Carlos

This paper first presents a definition of efficiency that 
can be applied indistinctly to heterogeneous and 
homogeneous systems. From that definitio, the paper also 
presents an extention of the isoefficiency method that 
permits its applications to heterogeneous environments as 
well. Finally, the paper presents some experiments when 
the methods' usefulness and validity are shown.
