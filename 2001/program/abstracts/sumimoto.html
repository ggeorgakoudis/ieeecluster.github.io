Experiences on a 1,024 Processor PC Cluster, RWC SCore Cluster III

Dr. Shinji Sumimoto
Real World Computing Partnership

RWC SCore Cluster III consists of 1,024 Pentium III
processors connected by Myrinet and two Fast Ethernet
links. It demonstrates that the SCore cluster system
software, developed at RWCP, utilizes 1,024 processors. In
his presentation, experiences on development and testing
he cluster, and its performance are shown.
