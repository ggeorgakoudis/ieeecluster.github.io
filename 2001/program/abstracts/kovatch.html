LosLobos: Administration Experience and Application Results

Patricia Kovatch
University of New Mexico, Albuquerque High Performance Computing Center

The Albuquerque High Performance Computing Center
(AHPCC) as a member of the National Computational Science
Alliance (NCSA) has designed several production Linux
superclusters for scientists and researchers to run a
variety of parallel applications. This poster will show
information on the design, implementation and management
of LosLobos, a 512 processor Linux cluster with a Myrinet
network. We will also feature some recent application results.
