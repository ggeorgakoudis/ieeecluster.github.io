TeraGrids: The Future of Terascale Cluster Computing 

Dan Reed
National Center for Supercomputing Applications 
University of Illinois
reed@ncsa.uiuc.edu

Commodity processors and storage (embodied in clusters), very high-performance WAN and LAN interconnects, Linux and open source HPC and Grid software are transforming the way we develop and deploy high-performance computing resources. Concurrently, a new model of nationally integrated, tightly coupled scientific data archives and instruments is emerging. This talk outlines a new worldview of distributed data, computing, and network resources for 21st century science and engineering. It concludes with a look at the future of cluster computing and some thoughts on the five and ten year futures.
