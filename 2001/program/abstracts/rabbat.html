A High-Availability Clustering Infrastructure with Data Integrity Guarantees 

Richard Rabbat 
rabbat@mclinux.com
Tom McNeal 
Tim Burke 
Mission Critical Linux

This paper discusses the design and implementation of a
highly-available clustering solution. This solution provides data
integrity guarantees and a simple and effective way for making services
highly available. The implementation of the system design in a Linux
environment on commodity hardware is documented. A proof- of-concept
to provide a highly available NFS server is explained with caveats and
their resolutions. Benchmarks are also provided that show the
applicability and effectiveness of such a system.
