Scalability and Performance of Two Large Linux Clusters 

Ron Brightwell and Steve Plimpton
Sandia National Laboratories

We present performance results from several parallel
benchmarks and applications on two large Linux clusters
at Sandia National Laboratories. We compare the results
on the Linux clusters to performance obtained on a
traditional distributed-memory massively parallel
processing machine, the Intel TeraFLOPS (ASCI/Red).
