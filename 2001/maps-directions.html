IEEE Cluster 2001: Maps and Directions:
Directions to the Sutton Place Hotel, Newport Beach, CA

From Central Orange Country/Downtown Los Angeles

Take the 5 Freeway South to the 55 Freeway South to the 405 South. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevard. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel parking lot.

From South Orange Country/San Diego

Take the 5 Freeway North to the 405 Freeway North. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevard. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel Parking lot.

From North Orange County/Long Beach/LAX/San Fernando Valley

Take the 405 Freeway South. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevard. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel parking lot.

From Pasadena

Take the 710 Freeway South to the 405 Freeway South. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevard. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel parking lot.

From Riverside

Take the 91 Freeway West to the 55 Freeway South to the 405 South. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevards. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel parking lot.

From Ontario

Take the 10 Freeway West to the 57 Freeway South to the 5 Freeway South to the 55 Freeway South to the 405 Freeway South. Exit and turn left on MacArthur Boulevard. Pass Birch Street and turn left into the first driveway. Follow the signs to the Sutton Place Hotel parking lot.
