Student Travel Grants  
   
Introduction
CLUSTER 2010 welcomes students from US-based universities to submit travel grant applications. We anticipate to defray travel/accommodation/food costs for a small number of students. As such, the travel grant is likely to be very competitive, and we encourage students to submit a carefully crafted travel grant application following the guidelines and deadlines mentioned below.
Requirements
  • Applicants must be registered students of US based universities.
  • Successful applicants will be required to submit a report of their experiences after the conference.
Instructions
  • Fill the application form. Pay particular attention to the paragraph about why the applicant wants to attend CLUSTER 2010.
  • Prepare your cv in pdf form. You will need to attach it in the form.
  • Submit the form by the deadline.
  • You can resubmit the form if you want to make changes. However, more than three submissions is not allowed.
  • For questions, please contact Dr. Ali Butt at butta at cs dot vt dot edu. Make sure to use a subject line of 'CLUSTER 2010 Travel Grant Question'
Deadlines
The deadline for receipt of the completed application form is 8/28/2010 6:00 p.m. US Eastern time.
