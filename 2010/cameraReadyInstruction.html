Camera-Ready Author Kit and Submission Instructions for Main Conference Papers  
   

Maximum paper lengths
10 pages
Extra pages
Up to 2 pages, fee per page is €150
Paper Style

All papers must have no page numbers and be formatted on US letter paper in 2 columns.

Formatting Instructions: 8.5" x 11" x 2 (DOC, PDF)
LaTeX and Word tempaltes

LaTeX authors should use the LaTex Formatting Macros provided by IEEE CPS.

Word authors should use the Word template provided by IEEE CPS.
Camera-ready paper submission
Paper authors should follow the instructions given in the CLUSTER 2010 author kit for preparing and submitting their camera-ready paper copies.
Conference registration
At least one author per paper must register by the camera-ready
paper deadline in order for the paper to appear in the conference proceedings. We look forward to meeting you in Greece!
