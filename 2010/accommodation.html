Venue & Accommodation  
   
Cluster 2010 will take place in Hersonissos, Crete, Greece. Hersonissos is a small town approximately 30km from Heraklion and its airport. For instructions of how to get to the conference venue, please visit the travel information section on the Cluster 2010 web page.

The venue of the conference is Aldemar Knossos Royal Village. Aldemar Knossos Royal Village hotel is a magnificent resort located on the north coast of the island of Crete in Hersonissos. The hotel has 364 rooms and 40 deluxe villas with all the contemporary amenities such as air-conditioning, direct dial telephone, safe, refrigerator, color satellite television, furnished balcony or veranda. Apart from its 13 swimming pools (seawater and/or fresh water), boutiques, kiosk, internet area, mini-market, hair salon etc. The facilities include special services for children, such as baby cots, babysitting, multi-lingual animation and mini-club with activities and programmes. The recreation area offers a mini-golf course, work-out programmes with fitness instructors, a giant outdoor chess board, large-screen TV & video room, beach volley facilities, 2 table tennis, evening programmes and shows by the animation team, open amphitheatre, game room etc.

Hotel rates for Cluster 2010 participants:
Special, low rates are offered to Cluster 2010 participants:

  • Single Room: 118,00 Euros.
  • Double Room: 147,00 Euros.
  • Triple Bungalow (with child 02-12 years): 147,00 Euros.
  • Triple Bungalow (with adult): 210,00 Euros.
  • Rates are per room, per day, including an American Buffet Breakfast in the Hotel's main restaurant. Vat and Tax are included.
  • The dead line for reservations is until 16/08/10. After 16/08/10 the reservations will be 'on request' basis.
  • Please specify that you are attending Cluster2010 to receive these rates.

Hotel payment and Cancellation Policy:
You will pay directly at the hotel. Cancellation details as below:

  • For all cancellations made by the participants until 30.08.2010 there will be no cancellation fees.
  • For all cancellations of the reserved rooms made from 31.08.2010 until 09.09.2010 there will be a charge equal to 1 overnight, for the cancelled rooms.
  • For all cancellations of the reserved rooms made from 10.09.2010 until 12.09.2010 there will be a charge equal to 2 overnights, for the cancelled rooms.
  • For all cancellations made the arrival day or/and for NON-SHOW guests, there will be a charge equal to the total reserved overnights.
