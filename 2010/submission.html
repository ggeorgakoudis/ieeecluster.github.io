Paper submission  
   

Paper submissions should demonstrate current research in any area of interest to Cluster2010. All submissions will be judged based on their correctness, originality, technical strength, significance, presentation, and relevance to the conference attendees. Submissions must represent original unpublished research that is not currently under review for any other conference or journal.

Papers should be submitted in PDF including author names and formatted using a double-column layout on US Letter (8.5in x 11in) paper; they may not exceed 10 pages, including everything ( figures, tables, references, appendices, etc.). All body text should be set in times 10pt type. You are encouraged to use the IEEE style file for Transactions, for which templates are available in both LaTeX and Word. Note that the body text when using the Word template must be set in times 10-point type. The margins, spacing, and font sizes should not be reduced from those specified by the style files. A PDF format checker will be used to verify compliance with these guidelines. Papers that violate any of them will be rejected, without any consideration of their merit.

The IEEE copyright notice is not required of submissions, only of accepted papers. Authors of accepted papers will be required to sign the IEEE copyright form.
