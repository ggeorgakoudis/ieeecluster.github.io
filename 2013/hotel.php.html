Cluster 2013 Hotel Accommodation

Conference Site and Hotel:  Hilton Indianapolis Hotel and Suites (Downtown)

IEEE Cluster 2013 attendees are encouraged to stay at the Hilton in Downtown Indianapolis (conference site):


120 West Market Street
Indianapolis, Indiana, United States 46204
Tel: 1-317-972-0600, Fax: 1-317-972-0660

The Hilton hotel is located in downtown Indianapolis, Indiana, and is the conference site for IEEE Cluster 2013. The Hilton is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Indianapolis and is within walking distance of these attractions; Circle Centre Mall, Monument Circle and the State Capitol, Lucas Oil Stadium, Banker’s Life Fieldhouse, Indianapolis Zoo, Eiteljorg Museum of American Indians and Western Art, Indiana State Museum, Mass Ave Arts District . Also nearby are the Children’s Museum and Indianapolis Museum of Art. See  - http://visitindy.com.
