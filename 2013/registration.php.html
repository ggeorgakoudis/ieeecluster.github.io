Letter of confirmation

If you need to request a letter of confirmation to assist with visa application, be sure that you have registered *and paid in full*. Then, send your request to iuconfs@indiana.edu, and provide the following information:

Full (given/legal) name

Legal address

Place/date of birth

Source of funding

Name/email of contact at Embassy (if available)

Once we have received this information, we can send a PDF signed letter confirming your registration.
