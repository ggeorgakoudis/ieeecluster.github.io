Cluster 2013 Tutorials - Clusters and cyberinfrastructure strategy

Monday, September 23, 2013

See Conference Program for time, room information, updated abstracts, and presenter information.

"The tutorials form a very important part of the education and professional development aspects of this conference. From the student perspective, tutorials are a great way for them to learn from today's top experts. For the academic and professional communities, tutorials are a great way to facilitate the careers of the top experts of tomorrow. " Craig Stewart, Executive Director, Pervasive Technology Institute, Associate Dean, Research Technologies.
