IEEE Cluster 2013 Conference deadlines extended


The deadline for early-bird registration and guaranteed hotel rates has been extended to August 31 for conference participants and will be extended for accepted workshop presenters once they are determined.

Workshop and Tutorials do not require conference registration but we do encourage everyone to attend all three - tutorials, conference, and workshops.

Panels will include:

  • Clouds and Clusters - the Walmart effect?  - Jim Bottum, Dennis Gannon, Thomas Sterling, Jay Boisseau (Abani Patra, moderator)
  • Research Data Alliance.  - Reagan Moore, Giridhar Manepalli (Robert H. McDonald, moderator)
  • The many pathways of Campus Bridging - Campus Bridging stories from four different instances.  Ian Foster, Scott Teige, Marcus Alfred, Tom Bishop (Richard Knepper, moderator)
