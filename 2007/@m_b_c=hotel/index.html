Hotel Reservations

Book Your Hotel Room at the Omni Austin Downtown by August 25! Conference attendees should reserve hotel rooms by or before Saturday, August 25, to secure the group rate. The conference hotel rate is $154 for individuals. PLEASE NOTE: The government rate is sold out. After August 25, the hotel has the option to continue to sell at the group rate or offer the best available rate at that time.

Please tell the agent that you are booking a room for the IEEE Cluster/Grid conference in September at the Austin Omni Downtown Hotel. This will ensure that you receive the group rate mentioned above. You will need a credit card to secure your reservation.
