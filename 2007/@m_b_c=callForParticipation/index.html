Grid 2007

Cluster 2007 welcomes paper and poster submissions on innovative work from researchers in academia, industry and national labs describing original research work in cluster computing. Topics of interest are but not limited to:

  • Adaptation of parallel algorithms to mutl-core cluster architecture
  • Cluster networking
  • Performance analysis an evaluation
  • Cluster management and maintenance
  • Cluster software and middleware
  • Storage and cluster I/O subsystems
  • Clustering for high availability
  • Applications
