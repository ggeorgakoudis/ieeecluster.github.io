Technical Papers

  • Accepted Papers -- 48 papers have been accepted to the conference and will be presented in 16 sessions with 2 parallel tracks. See the preliminary program for a precise schedule.

  • Proceedings -- The proceedings will be made available on CD-ROM and be part of the IEEE on-line proceeding library.

  • Journal Special Issue -- Authors of the best papers from the conference will be invited to submit an expanded version of their papers for possible publication in a special issue of the International Journal of High Performance Computing and Networking (IJHPCN), which will appear in 2005.

  • Questions -- Questions should be sent fo Phillip Papadopoulos phil@sdsc.edu, the conference's Program Chair, and to Remy Evard evard@mcs.anl.gov, the Deputy Program Chair.
